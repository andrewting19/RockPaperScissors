Rules

The game of P, R, S is based on trust, skill and determination. Each player must:
  • Pick a throw
  • Pray to their deity of choice
  • Receive the spoils of war, or BE the spoils of war
If you ever feel insignificant and worthless, just remember these three things:
  • Rock > Scissors
  • Scissors > Paper
  • Paper > Rock
If you're getting serious about RPS, check out the World RPS Society!
